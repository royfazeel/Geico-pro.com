Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GEICO insurance and our service.

What is GEICO-Pro.com?+
GEICO-Pro.com is an independent guidance service that helps customers understand GEICO's insurance products. We are not affiliated with GEICO. We provide information, help compare coverage, and connect you with specialists.
What types of insurance does GEICO offer?+
GEICO offers auto, homeowners, renters, condo, motorcycle, ATV, boat, RV, life, umbrella, pet, travel, business, commercial auto, identity theft protection, flood, earthquake, and event insurance.
How much can I save?+
On average, customers save $500+ per year. GEICO's average rates are ~21% below the national average. Additional savings through bundling, safe driver, military, student, and other discounts.
Is GEICO available in my state?+
GEICO provides auto insurance in all 50 states and DC. Property products are widely available through partner companies. Call (888) 524-0250 to check availability.
How do I file a claim?+
GEICO offers 24/7 claims service via their mobile app, website, or phone. The app lets you track claims, upload photos, and communicate with adjusters.
Can I bundle policies?+
Yes! Bundle auto + home, renters, condo, or motorcycle for up to 25% savings on your total premiums.
Is calling free?+
Yes, our service is completely free. No obligation, no pressure. Our specialists are happy to answer questions and help you understand your options.
What is the DriveEasy® program?+
DriveEasy is an optional app-based program that tracks driving habits like speed, braking, and phone use. Safe drivers earn personalized discounts. Not available in all states.
