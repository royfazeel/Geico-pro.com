Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GEICO insurance and our guidance service.

What is GEICO-Pro.com?+
GEICO-Pro.com is an independent guidance and comparison service that helps customers understand GEICO's insurance products. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by GEICO. We provide information, help you compare coverage options, and connect you with specialists for personalized assistance.
What types of insurance does GEICO offer?+
GEICO offers a wide range of insurance products including auto, homeowners, renters, condo, motorcycle, ATV, boat, RV, life, umbrella, pet, travel, business, commercial auto, and identity theft protection. They also offer specialty products like flood, earthquake, and event insurance through partner companies.
How much can I save with GEICO?+
Savings vary depending on your coverage, location, and driving history. On average, GEICO customers report saving $500 or more per year on auto insurance. Additional savings are available through multi-policy discounts, safe driver discounts, military discounts, and more.
What discounts are available?+
GEICO offers numerous discounts including multi-policy bundles (up to 25%), safe driver discount (up to 26%), good student discount (up to 15%), military and federal employee special rates, multi-vehicle discount, paid-in-full discount, defensive driving course discount, and the DriveEasy safe driving program.
Is GEICO available in my state?+
GEICO provides auto insurance in all 50 states and the District of Columbia. Homeowners, renters, and other property insurance products are also widely available through GEICO Insurance Agency partners. Call us at (888) 524-0250 to check availability.
How do I file a claim?+
GEICO offers 24/7 claims service through multiple channels. You can file a claim through the GEICO mobile app, on geico.com, or by calling GEICO directly. The app also lets you track claim status, upload photos, and communicate with your adjuster.
Can I bundle multiple policies?+
Yes! GEICO offers a multi-policy discount when you combine multiple insurance products. Common bundles include auto + homeowners, auto + renters, auto + condo, and auto + motorcycle. Bundling can save you up to 25% on your total premiums.
Does calling GEICO-Pro.com cost anything?+
No, our service is completely free. We provide information and guidance at no cost to you. There is no obligation when you call, and our specialists are happy to answer your questions.
