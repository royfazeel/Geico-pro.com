Auto Coverage at a Glance

Understanding what's included in your auto policy helps you make smarter decisions. Here's what to compare.

🛡️

Liability Coverage

Required in most states. Pays for injury and property damage you cause to others in an at-fault accident.

💥

Collision Coverage

Covers damage to your own vehicle from collisions with other cars or objects, regardless of fault.

🌧️

Comprehensive Coverage

Protects against non-collision events: theft, vandalism, hail, flood, fire, falling objects, and animal strikes.

🚑

Medical Payments

Covers medical bills for you and your passengers after an accident, regardless of who caused it.

🚗

Rental Reimbursement

Pays for a rental car while your vehicle is being repaired after a covered claim. A small add-on that saves big hassle.

🛣️

Roadside Assistance

24/7 help for towing, flat tires, dead batteries, fuel delivery, and lockout services. Peace of mind on every trip.

👤

Uninsured Motorist

Protects you if you're hit by a driver with no insurance or insufficient coverage to pay your claim.

📱

Usage-Based Discounts

Some carriers offer app-based programs that track safe driving habits and reward you with lower premiums.

Homeowners Coverage Explained

Your home is likely your most valuable asset. Here's what quality homeowners insurance should cover.

🏗️

Dwelling Protection

Covers the physical structure of your home from fire, wind, hail, lightning, and other covered perils.

📦

Personal Property

Protects your belongings — furniture, electronics, clothing, jewelry — from theft, fire, and covered events.

⚖️

Liability Coverage

Covers legal costs and damages if someone is injured on your property or you accidentally damage someone else's property.

🏨

Loss of Use

Pays for temporary living expenses (hotel, food, etc.) if your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered event.

🏚️

Other Structures

Covers detached structures like garages, fences, sheds, and pool houses on your property.

💰

Bundle Savings

Many carriers offer up to 25% discount when you bundle home insurance with auto — a quick way to save on both.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our service and insurance comparison.

What is GEICO-Pro.com and is it the official GEICO site?+
No, we are NOT the official GEICO website. GEICO-Pro.com is an independent comparison and referral platform. We help consumers compare insurance options and connect with licensed specialists. The official GEICO website is geico.com. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by GEICO.
How is this service free?+
We are compensated by insurance providers when a policy is placed through our referral. This allows us to offer free, no-obligation comparison assistance to consumers. You are never charged for calling or using our service.
What information do I need when I call?+
Having these items handy helps: current policy details (if switching), driver's license number, vehicle information (year/make/model), your ZIP code, and a general idea of your coverage needs. Don't worry if you don't have everything — our specialists can still help.
Can I manage my existing policy through this site?+
No. We do not manage existing policies, handle claims, process payments, or provide account access for any insurance company. To manage an existing policy, please contact your insurer directly through their official website or customer service number.
How long does the comparison process take?+
Most callers are presented with comparison options within 10–15 minutes. The entire process — from calling to potentially binding a new policy — can often be completed in a single phone call if you choose to proceed.
What types of insurance can I compare?+
Auto, homeowners, renters, condo, motorcycle, umbrella, life, boat, RV, commercial auto, and bundled policies. Our specialists can also help with specialty coverage needs.
Is my information safe?+
Yes. We take data security seriously. We do not sell your personal information to unrelated third parties. Information you provide is used solely to help connect you with relevant insurance options. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Am I obligated to buy anything?+
Absolutely not. Our service is 100% no-obligation. If none of the options presented appeal to you, simply thank the specialist and hang up. There is never any pressure to purchase.
